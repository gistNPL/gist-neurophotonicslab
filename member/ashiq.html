Research & Technical Skills

Wet Lab

  • Primary Neuron Culture: Dissection and in vitro culture of adult cortical & spinal neurons
  • Organotypic Slice Preparation: Handling, slicing, and culturing brain tissue(s) on MEA platforms
  • Electrophysiology (HD-MEA): High-density recordings; analysis of LFPs, MFR, burst activity, and network synchrony
  • Calcium & Voltage Imaging: Dye loading (Fluo-4 AM, FluoVolt); live-cell imaging and signal acquisition optimization
  • Microscopy: Wide-field/light-field/confocal operation; sample mounting and fluorescence bead calibration
  • Immunostaining & Histology: Cryosectioning, immunostaining, IHC; segmentation-based analysis (SAM, Cellpose)
  • Tracer Injection & Perfusion: In vivo vascular/CSF tracing; drug perfusion for BBB/vascular studies
  • Behavioral Analysis: OFT, nesting, social interaction, MoSeq, von Frey; automated pipelines (DeepLabCut, YOLOv8)

Computing

  • Programming & Data Analysis: Python, R; neural signal processing, behavioral analysis, image analysis, ML (PCA, clustering, change point detection, SHAP)
  • Neuroimaging & Signal Processing: MATLAB (incl. Simulink), SciPy, OpenCV; PyTorch/TensorFlow for DL workflows
  • Microscopy & Imaging Analysis: Automated pipelines for stack processing, ΔF/F₀ computation, and 3D reconstruction (DeepCAD, Cellpose, SAM, U-Net)
  • Statistics & Visualization: seaborn, matplotlib, scikit-learn, statsmodels; publication-ready figures
